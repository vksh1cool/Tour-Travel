ITINERARY

    Day 01

  • Your wonderful Varanasi tour with Muskan Holidays will start with your arrival at the Varanasi airport/railway station (whichever of your choice).
  • At the airport/station, you will be greeted by our representative.
  • After getting freshened up, you will be served with a delectable lunch at the hotel.
  • Followed by lunch, you will be taken on a sightseeing tour of Varanasi.
  • On the tour, you will visit some top tourist attractions of Varanasi like Annapurna Temple, Vishwanath Temple, Sankat Mochan (Hanuman Temple), Bharat Mata Temple, Banaras Hindu University (BHU) and Manas Mandir.
  • After the sightseeing tour, in the evening, be ready to witness a surreal sight of Ganga Aarti on the banks of River Ganges.
  • Vist BANARAS Station. The newly transformed station now has 8 platforms including a second entry PF, which is truly world-class. It has a spacious waiting area, circulating area, booking/reservation office, cafeteria, food court, waiting rooms and more. The station also has AC lounge, non-AC retiring rooms and dormitories.
  • For dinner and overnight stay, you will be taken back to the same hotel.

    Day 02

  • The day will start early with an early morning boat ride on the waters of River Ganges.
  • This boat ride allows you to relish the beautiful sights of people offering water to Sun God along with taking a holy dip in the water of the river.
  • Through the boat ride, you will also be taken to all the popular Ghats of Varanasi.
  • After returning to the hotel, you will be served with a delicious breakfast.
  • Post breakfast, be ready to go out for another Varanasi sightseeing tour.
